BROWN BEAR

I always wanted to fold a bear in origami, but by 2024 (the year when I was looking for bear models) I found very few designs with diagrams that I truly liked. Perhaps the best ones I found were the bears by Quentin Trollip; however, I also wanted to achieve something in the style of Nicolás Gajardo and Shuki Kato. At the beginning of 2025, I set out to design my own brown bear, using some of Kamiya's proportions as a guide, especially those he used in his cattle model. I conceived the entire design while traveling in Medellín around mid-April. This is the second version of the design, updated in December 2025. I could retire from the world of origami and feel completely satisfied having my own origami bear.
